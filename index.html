Help

  • Each active player gets 3 cards.
  • Draw 1 card from stock/discard, then discard to 3 cards.
  • Score is best same-suit total. A=11, face cards=10.
  • Three-of-a-kind scores 30.5.
  • Exactly 31 ends the round immediately.
  • After a knock, everyone else gets one final turn.
  • Lowest hand loses 1 life. Failed knocker loses 2.
